1. Place the cursor in the text area located in the center of the WordPad window.
2. Type the text "Hello World!" in the text area.
3. Click on the "File" menu item located at the top left corner.
4. Click on the "Save as" menu item in the File menu.
5. In the "Save As" dialog window, type "doc" in the "File name" input field.
6. Click the "Save" button located at the bottom right of the "Save As" dialog window.
